Who

We are a grass root initiative of modellers from various universities and research institutes across Europe.

View members »

Aim

Open models and open data will advance knowledge and lead to better energy policies. Open up energy models improves quality, transparency, and credibility, leading to better research and policy advice.

View manifesto »

What

The openmod initiative is a network, an interest group, and a platform. We exchange ideas and source code, lobby for policy support for open projects, and actively share data, code and knowhow.

Future

Second workshop on Open Source / Open Data energy modeling on April 13/14, 2015 We will reconvene to meet for our second workshop at MCC in Berlin. Attached to the workshop there will be a modelling workshop hosted by Strommarkttreffen-techtalk (April 15/16), just before the Enerday conference in Dresden April 17), hosted by TU Dresden. The workshop will be in English language and we explicitly welcome participants from outside Germany, targeting a more balanced geographical representation.
